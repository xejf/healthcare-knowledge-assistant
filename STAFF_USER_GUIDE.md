# Staff Knowledge Assistant — Quick User Guide

*Sunrise Valley Health Clinic · Internal use only*

## What is it?

The Staff Knowledge Assistant is an internal chat tool that answers your work questions using **only our approved clinic documents** — the same onboarding guides, procedures, and policies you'd otherwise search by hand. It is for **staff only**, not for patients, and it never gives medical advice.

## How to use it — 3 steps

1. **Open the app** in your browser (ask your supervisor for the link).
2. **Type your question in your own words**, like you'd ask a colleague. No special wording needed.
3. **Read the answer and check the source.** Every answer shows which document it came from — click "Sources" to see it, or read the full document on the *Knowledge Base Viewer* page.

## What can I ask?

| Topic | Example |
|---|---|
| Onboarding | "What do I do on my first day?" |
| Appointments | "A patient wants to move her visit — what do I do?" |
| Billing direction | "A patient is upset about a bill, what can I tell them?" |
| Patient service | "How do I handle a complaint?" |
| Safety | "How do I report a wet floor?" |
| Escalation | "When should I get my supervisor involved?" |

## What it will NOT answer — and that's on purpose

The assistant always refuses questions about **diagnosis, treatment, medication, dosage, emergencies, insurance approvals, and legal matters**. If you ask one, it will tell you to contact your supervisor or the relevant department. This is a safety rule, not an error — those decisions belong to licensed clinical staff, the Billing Department, or management.

⚠️ **In a real emergency, never use the assistant.** Follow the emergency protocol: alert the charge nurse (ext. 2500) or call 911.

## Good to know

- **"I could not find this information…"** means our approved documents don't cover it. Ask your shift supervisor — and suggest the topic be added.
- **A yellow warning** under an answer means the match was weak — double-check with your supervisor before acting.
- The assistant **never guesses or invents procedures.** If it answered, the information came from an approved document, and the source is shown.
- Unsure about anything? **When in doubt, ask your supervisor.** The assistant is a helper, not a replacement for human judgment.

## Problems with the app?

If it shows an error or seems unavailable, tell your shift supervisor or call the IT helpdesk (ext. 2400).
